# Concepts and Bases of Information Security

## 信息安全的三要素

- Condentiality
- Integrity (非法修改数据)
- Availability

## Types of security attack
### Interruption
- Attack —— "Availability"
- Easy to detect
- Ways of interruption
    - DOS
    - DDOS

### Interception
- Attack —— "Confidentiality"
- Difficult to detect a silent Interceptor

### Modification
- Attack —— "Integrity"
- Can be prevented

### Fabrication
- Attack —— "Authenticity"


## 分类
- Passive Attacks
    - Goal: interception
    - Difficult to detect
- Active Attacks
    - Easy to detect
    - Hard to prevent
